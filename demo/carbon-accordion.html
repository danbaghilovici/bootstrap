Example of using the accordion in your HTML:

  <div class="accordion-item">
    <h2 class="accordion-header">
      <button class="accordion-button">...</button>

    <div class="accordion-collapse collapse">
      <div class="accordion-body">...</div>
    </div>
  </div>
