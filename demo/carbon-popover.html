Carbon Popover Demo

Popover component styled with Carbon Design System specifications.

Popover Placements

Popovers can be positioned on top, bottom, left, or right of the trigger element.

Body Only (No Header)

Popover with content only, no title/header.

With Header and Body

Popover with both a title (header) and content (body).

Long Content (Max Width: 368px)

Popover with long content to demonstrate the max-width constraint of 368px.

Dismissible Popover

Click anywhere outside the popover to dismiss it.

Dismissible popover

HTML Structure Reference

Copy this HTML structure to use the popover component.

<!-- Basic popover trigger -->
<button type="button" class="btn btn-primary"
        data-bs-toggle="popover"
        data-bs-placement="right"
        data-bs-title="Popover Title"
        data-bs-content="Popover content goes here.">
  Click me
</button>

<!-- Body only (no header) -->
<button type="button" class="btn btn-secondary"
        data-bs-toggle="popover"
        data-bs-content="Body content only, no title.">
  No header
</button>

<!-- Dismissible popover -->
<a tabindex="0" class="btn btn-danger" role="button"
   data-bs-toggle="popover"
   data-bs-trigger="focus"
   data-bs-title="Dismissible"
   data-bs-content="Click outside to close.">
  Dismissible
</a>
